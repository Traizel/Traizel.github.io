What is TraizelCraft

The TraizelCraft Minecraft Server is a 10+ year Open World RPG with a immersive story, lore, secrets, and tons of history

Help us out!

You can help us improve the server! If you find a bug or exploit, we have a channel on our discord that players can report in! Post anything wrong with the server in there. We also allow players to anonymously report other players of breaking rules and being toxic. Simply report the player in the same channel!

What sets us apart

TraizelCraft is unique and there is no other server like it. That is saying a lot, but I assure anyone who plays the server can agree. We have a massive Open World RPG map that has been in the works for 10+ years and is will having addiitons being made. We have hundereds of secrets, lore, easter eggs, fun facts, and so much history. We also offer players to download old versions to explore the history!

You can spend hours upon hours discovering the map, and that is only the RPG! ON TOP of the RPG, we have Dungeons, Factions, Parkour, Mazes, and more, all with a twist. We have a complex leveling system simlar to Skyrim or the Witcher. We have hundereds of cusotm mobs and bosses. We have hundereds of gear to collect and a complex loot system similar to Destiny. This is your dream server. This is TraizelCraft.
