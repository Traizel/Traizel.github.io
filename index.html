Experience the massive story of TraizelCraft

TraizelCraft Network has many different servers all with tons of gameplay and lore.

TraizelCraft was started back in 2012 on the Xbox 360 edition of Minecraft by a guy named Traizel. Over the years, his great creation grew into something he wanted to show off. One day, that became a reality when he started to show players his massive build. It only took a few months until several people were in love with his creation and wanted to see more.

Traizel continued to build not only his Minecraft world, but what was going to become a growing community of wonderful people. Today, TraizelCraft Network has grown substantially, and currently has a massive RPG Survival server with tons of story and lore, a Factions server for the hardcore PvP players, and a Mini Games server for fun with friends.

The Incredible RPG Survival Server

The RPG Survival server is by far the biggest project on TraizelCraft. We plan to tell the story of the Traveler through the RPG.

You can visit our Wiki for more information on the story. The RPG has more beyond the story and lore. There are difficult dungeons, unique parkour maps, mazes, tons of quests to complete, secrets to find, and many survival maps to build your dream home!

The Competitive side of TraizelCraft

The Factions server is one giant map for players to build their empires and start wars.

Anyone who enjoys PvP will have a ton of fun on Factions. Fight against many other factions and raid others, claim more land, and grow to the top leaderboards each season! There are multiple game modes and events in the Factions server. You can do some Parkour, complete many quests, participate in server events like envoys, and more!
